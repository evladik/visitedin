Privacy Policy

LinkedIn Profile Tracker Chrome Extension

Our Commitment: Your privacy is important to us. This extension stores all data locally on your device and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

Local Data Storage Only

The LinkedIn Profile Tracker extension collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • LinkedIn Profile IDs: Unique identifiers extracted from LinkedIn profile URLs you visit
  • Visit Timestamps: When you visited each profile (for internal tracking purposes)
  • Profile Count Statistics: Total number of profiles you've visited

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (names, emails, phone numbers)
  • LinkedIn profile content or data
  • Browsing history outside of LinkedIn profile visits
  • Any data that could identify you personally
  • Screenshots or images from LinkedIn

2. How We Use Your Information

The locally stored data is used exclusively to:

  • Display visual indicators on LinkedIn profiles you've previously visited
  • Provide statistics about your profile viewing activity
  • Improve your browsing experience by preventing duplicate profile visits

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only

  • All data is stored locally using Chrome's secure storage API
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No data is shared with the extension developer
  • No data is shared with LinkedIn or any third parties

Data Security

  • Data is encrypted using Chrome's built-in security mechanisms
  • Only accessible by the extension itself
  • Protected by your device's security settings

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

No Data Sharing: We do not share, sell, trade, or transfer any of your data to third parties. All data remains on your device.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Data Retention

  • Data is stored indefinitely until you choose to delete it
  • Data persists across browser sessions
  • Data is removed if you uninstall the extension

How to Delete Your Data

You can delete all stored data at any time by:

  • Clicking the extension icon and selecting "Clear All Data"
  • Uninstalling the extension from Chrome
  • Clearing Chrome extension data in browser settings

6. Permissions Explanation

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • storage: To save visited profile data locally on your device
  • activeTab: To detect when you're on a LinkedIn page and show relevant statistics
  • *://*.linkedin.com/*: To operate only on LinkedIn pages and add visual indicators

7. Changes to LinkedIn's Website

This extension operates by analyzing LinkedIn's publicly available page structure. We do not:

  • Access private LinkedIn data
  • Bypass LinkedIn's privacy settings
  • Store any LinkedIn content or user data
  • Interfere with LinkedIn's normal operation

8. Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. LinkedIn's terms of service require users to be at least 16 years old.

9. International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers involved with this extension.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (view statistics in the extension popup)
  • Delete your data (clear all data option)
  • Control data collection (disable/uninstall the extension)
  • Know what data we collect (outlined in this policy)
